Amanda Nunes: Queen of the Octagon

Join us for an intimate conversation with the undisputed G.O.A.T. of women's MMA, Amanda Nunes. The Lioness reflects on her incredible journey, the challenges she's overcome, and what it means to be a double-champion. "Every fight has taught me something new," Nunes shares. "It's not just about winning; it's about continuously improving and proving myself against the best."

We discuss her unique training methods, the sacrifices required to stay at the top, and her life as a mother and wife outside the intense world of professional fighting. She provides insights into her mental preparation and how she handles the immense pressure of championship bouts. "Discipline is key, inside and outside the cage," she emphasizes. "My family is my biggest motivation, and they help me stay grounded."

Nunes also touches on the future of women's MMA and her role in inspiring the next generation of fighters. "I hope my legacy is not just about titles, but about showing young girls that they can achieve anything if they work hard and believe in themselves."
