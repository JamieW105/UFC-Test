Jon Jones: Heavyweight Dominance

We caught up with the UFC Heavyweight Champion, Jon "Bones" Jones, to discuss his successful transition to the heavyweight division and his ambitions to further cement his status as the greatest of all time. "The move to heavyweight was a natural evolution for me," Jones explains. "I've always envisioned myself dominating this division, and now I'm here."

Jones shares details about his increased strength and conditioning regimen, adapting his renowned fighting style to the larger weight class, and the new challenges and opportunities he sees at heavyweight. He reflects on his career, the controversies, and his renewed focus on legacy. "Every fight is a chance to prove myself anew," he says. "I'm constantly evolving, and I believe my best performances are still ahead of me."

The interview also touches on his current training camp, potential future opponents, and his mindset as he looks to defend his title against the division's elite. "I'm here to clean out the division," Jones asserts. "I'm ready for anyone they put in front of me."
